# Decentralized Finance

In this campaign, you'll explore decentralized finance (DeFi) and its various sectors. You'll learn about decentralized exchanges, liquidity pools, and lending/borrowing protocols. Specifically, you'll gain hands-on experience with Uniswap for swapping cryptocurrencies and become familiar with Aave for lending and borrowing. By the end of the campaign, you'll have a solid understanding of DeFi and be able to interact with DeFi protocols effectively.
